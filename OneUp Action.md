- # OneUp Action
  **Subtype** Social
  **Tags:** Community
  **Summary:**We’re an intersectional youth-led organization working to provide resources to youth advocates.
  **Deployment Status:**
  **Description:**OneUpAction is built by a group of powerful intersectional youth advocates who realized that just participating in climate strikes, rallies, and legislative advocacy wasn’t enough to solve the climate crisis. In order to see a true impact, we must take our energy to mass mobilize and complement it with local direct action. They are the founding members of OneUpAction with the goal of creating a platform in which marginalized young people could get the resources needed to be empowered to take direct action in their own communities to combat the climate crisis when people in positions of power are not. OneUpAction's progams include the Action Incubator Fellowship, the Youth Innovators Fellowship, and Youth Solutionists in Government.
